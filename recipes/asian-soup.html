Asian Noodle Soup

Description

Asian noodle soup is a comforting and flavorful dish that brings together rich umami flavors with a satisfying balance of textures. The broth, made from a savory blend of chicken stock, soy sauce, and oyster sauce, creates a deep, slightly sweet, and salty base that coats every ingredient with richness. Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 3 green onions finely sliced into rounds
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon low sodium dark soy sauce
  • 4 oz dried chinese noodles of choice
  • 4 leaves bok choy
