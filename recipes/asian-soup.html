Asian Noodle Soup

Description

Asian noodle soup is a comforting and flavorful dish that brings together rich umami flavors with a satisfying balance of textures. The broth, made from a savory blend of chicken stock, soy sauce, and oyster sauce, creates a deep, slightly sweet, and salty base that coats every ingredient with richness. Whether using tender slices of chicken or beef, the protein absorbs the brothâ€™s essence, enhancing its taste with every bite. The noodles, soft yet slightly chewy, soak up the flavors while adding a hearty and filling component to the soup.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 3 green onions finely sliced into rounds
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon low sodium dark soy sauce
  • 4 oz dried chinese noodles of choice
  • 4 leaves bok choy

Instructions

  1. In a large saucepan, heat the chicken stock and bring to a boil.
  2. In another saucepan, poach the chicken tender.
  3. Add the green onions, bok choy, green onions, soy sauces, oyster suace, chicken, and noodles.
  4. Reduce the heat and cook according to noodle package instructions.
