Spaghetti

Description

This dish is perfect for a cozy family meal or a simple yet satisfying dinner. The combination of savory beef and seasoned tomato sauce creates a classic Italian-inspired flavor profile, made even better with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese or fresh herbs for extra depth. Whether served fresh off the stove or reheated the next day, your spaghetti with ground beef remains a timeless, comforting favorite that fills the kitchen with an irresistible aroma and brings everyone to the table.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlics, minced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 teaspons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper in a large saucepan over a medium-high heat.
  3. Cook and stir saucepan until meat is browned and crumbly and vegetables are tender. About 5-7 minutes.
  4. Drain grease.
  5. Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into the pan. Season with oregano basil, salt, and pepper.
  6. Simmer spaghetti for 1 hour, stirring ocassionally.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
